Privilege Spectrum

The game to understand privilege and help you take action.

What is Privilege Spectrum?

An interactive tool that helps you explore the various forms of privilege that shape our experiences. Through thoughtful questions, you'll gain insight into how different aspects of identity and circumstance create advantages or challenges in our society.

How It Works

1

Answer Questions

Respond to a series of questions about your life experiences. Each question explores different aspects of privilege in society.

2

See Your Results

Your responses are calculated to show where you fall on the privilege spectrum, revealing patterns of advantage and disadvantage.

3

Take Action

Receive guidance on using your privilege constructively—whether amplifying voices, advocating for change, or building supportive communities.

Why It Matters

Understanding privilege isn't about guilt—it's about awareness. Recognizing our advantages and challenges helps us become better allies, make informed decisions, and work toward equity. When we understand how systems of privilege operate, we can actively contribute to creating a more just and inclusive society.

Real Impact

Used by 255 Amazonians in November 2025

Featured at an Amazon diversity event where participants explored privilege awareness in a safe, interactive environment. The experience sparked meaningful conversations and helped build a more inclusive workplace culture.
